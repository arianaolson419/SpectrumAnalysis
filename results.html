Results

Authors: Ariana Olson, Harper Owen, Paige Pfenninger

Look at the cool videos!

We reached all of our learning goals over the course of this project. The GUI was functional, we implementated color modulation, and the sculpture has a real time music player and visualizer. Future work may include using libraries to map color to frequency, mood, tempo, or beat of the song. Implementing love music analysis is another avenue of future work

The application of this sculpture are mainly education. It could be used to show the difference between styles of music of certain aspects of songs (such as the chorus, bridge, and verses). It could also potentially be used as a tool to analyze the frequencies of various types of human communication such as speeches or public conversations.
