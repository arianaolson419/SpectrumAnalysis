Our Story

Description

Our vision of the final project was not always an LED sculpture. At first, we planned on implementing our own Fast Fourier Transform from scratch and creating a visualizer in pygame. The LED sculpture and the GUI were stretch goals. However, we pivoted when we decided our interests were in visuals more than the math. The LED sculpture became the focus of the project after the pivot.
