Music Visualizer

Authors: Ariana Olson, Harper Owen, Paige Pfenninger

Description

Just as Fast Fourier Transform takes audio from the time domain to the frequency domain, we take music from the audio domain to the visual domain with our LED Music Visualizer. Visualizing audio with our program is as easy as choosing a song, pressing start, and watching it go. The frequency data of a song is displayed in a beautiful helical structure. The visulaizer comes with a variety of prelodaed songs that the user can choose from, but the real beauty of this project is that it is customizable.
